Ruminate Ventures

We help student entrepreneurs in the Midwest build and run their own startups Join our platform

We believe that midwest is an untapped market

The midwest is home to some of the world's best engineering schools. This region contains students with talent, pedigree and drive. Yet, it is overlooked. They don't get the same funding, time, and respect as students who are building companies on the coasts. We want to change that!

Where do we fit in?

Our goal is to help push forward an ecosystem of entrepenurship and innovation around midwest schools. Using our platform, we would like to cross-school collaboration among students who are intereseted in entrepneurship.
